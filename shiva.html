Asalu class loo evaru good person ga anipisthundhi and good knowledge vundhi ani nannu adigithe, naaku first gurthukochedhi Shiva.
Nijaniki chess loo and coding loo thanaku vunnantha knowledge inka evariki vundadhu annattuga anipisthu vuntundhi.
Asalu endhuku mana class loo Shiva ni like chesthaaru ante thanaku konni hidden skills vunnai. Avi entante:

Hidden skills:-
-> Evaraina emaina chebuthunte thanu thana work pakkana petti mari vere vaallu chepedhi vintaadu, ante thanau entha listening skills vundho think cheyyandi.
-> Thanu class loo eppudu kuda batti kottadaniki try cheyadu, edhaina own ga nerchukuntaadu, and nerchukundhi adagakamunche padhi mandhiki share chesthaadu.
-> Thanu entha baaga explain chesthaadante okasari cheppagane malli doubts rananthaga.
-> Enni maatalu annakaani thanu angry ga feel avvadu endhukante thanu edhi cheppina correct ga artham chesukuntaadu and friendship ki value isthaadu.
-> Coding naake baaga vachu ane proud thanalo vundadhu.
-> Evaraina problems loo vunte nakendhukule ani anukokunda help cheyyadaniki mathram venukadadu.
-> Normal ga seminar isthunte inka eppudu ayipothundha ani chusthaamu but thanu mathram andhariki artam ayyindha ledha ani chusthaadu.

Good habits:-
-> Studies ki entha preference isthaado friendship ki kuda anthe preference isthaadu.
-> Normal ga manam edhaina nerchukunte happy but thanaku mathram edhaina padhi mandhiki share chesthene happy.
-> Eppudu ela matladaalo, ela behave cheyaalo thanaku baaga thelusu.
-> Chaala hard-work chese person.
-> Andharilo positive ni mathrame chusthaadu.
-> Nijaniki thanu andharini encourage cheyadanike try chesthaadu.

Bad habits:-
-> Nijam cheppalante thanaku elanti bad habits levu.

Nick name:- "KNOWLEDGE GURU" (means:- his expertise in coding and chess, and his nature of sharing knowledge selflessly).
