Asalu class loo intha varaku angry ga vundadam chusaava ani nannu evaraina adigithe naaku first gurthukochedhi Rakshitha.
Nijaniki thanu 1st year nunchi lab loo naa lab friend hee.
Thanu lab loo side-by-side friend ayina, communication problem valla thanu naaku konchem late ga friend ayyindhi.
Thanani chusinappudalla nakokati anipisthundhi, adhentante asalu class loo intha manchi valllu vuntaara ani.
Endhukante 1st year nunchi thanani nenu emi adiginaa kuda thana noti nunchi "okay" or "yes" ane maate vachindhi kaani "no" ane maata raledhu.
Thanu imadhya naaku konchem close ga friend ayyindhi.
So appudappudu anukuntu vuntaanu, asalu thanatho 1st year nunchi sarigga matladithe bagundedhi ani, endhukani nenu thanatho intha late ga friendship chesaanu ani feel avuthaanu.
Thanaku emi anipisthe adhi matladedhi kadhu, ee time ki edhi matladaalo baaga alochinche matladuthundhi.
Nijaniki nenu appudappudu chaala sarlu sad ga feel avuthu vuntaanu endhukante, thana academic performance konchem low ani naaku thelusu,so naaku thelisi kuda nenu endhuku 1st year nunchi thanaku help cheyaledhu, thana naa pakkane kurchuntundhi kadha, mari ayina kuda nenu thanaku endhuku help cheyalekapothunna ani nenu feel avuthu vuntaanu.

Good habits:-
-> Thanu andharithonu friendly ga move avvadanike try chesthundhi.
-> Evaritho enthalo vundaalo, evaritho ela behave cheyyalo thanaku baaga thelusu.
-> Friendship ki value isthundhi.

Bad habits:-
-> Class loo and lab loo konchem silent gaa anipisthundhi, so ala kakunda konchem andharithonu free ga move ayithe inka better ani naa feeling.
