Nijaniki thanu naatho 1st and 2nd year loo pedhaga matladedhi kadhu, but 3rd year loo thanu imadhye konchem matladuthundhi.
1st year seminar ki and 3rd year seminar ki thana communication and talking skill chaala baaga improve chesukundhi.
Imadhya thana seminars vini nene shock ayya, asalu thanu antha perfect ga ela ivvagaligindhi ani.
So inka ilane thana skills ni improve chesukuntu velthe thanu top position lo vuntundhani naa feeling.

Good habits:-
-> Thanadhi friendly nature.
-> Manchi photographer kuda.
-> Eppudu ela behave cheyalo thanaku baaga thelusu.
-> Thanu ekkuvaga friends tho comedy ga move avadaniki like chesthundhi.

Bad habits:-
-> Thanu eppudu chusina last bench vaalla thone kanipisthundhi, so ala kakunda class andharithonu konchem close ga move avuthe inka better ani naa feeling.

Nick name:- "The Silent Achiever" (meaning:- his ability to silently improve and surprise with his skills).
