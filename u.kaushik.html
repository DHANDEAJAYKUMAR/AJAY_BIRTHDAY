Nijaniki thanu maata midha nilabade person.
Evaraina emaina cheppagane ventane artham chesukuntaadu, ante thanaku listening skills enthala vundho think cheyandi.
Oka person cheppe matalani thanu correct way lo artham chesukuntaadu.

-> Thanu friendship ki entha value isthado studies ki kuda anthe value isthaadu.
-> Helping nature kuda ekkuve.

-> Thanu evaripaina kuda depend avvadu.
-> Thanu work chesukunetappudu evarini disturb cheyadu, thanu disturb avvadu.

Bad habits:

Nickname: "THE LOGICAL LEADER" (emphasizing his ability to think logically and lead without being selfish).
