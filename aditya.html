Asalu class loo evaru face to face matlade person ante naaku first gurthukochedhi Aditya.
Endhukante thanu appudappudu edhutivaallu emi anukuntaaro ani kuda alochinchakunda direct ga anesthaaru.
Thanu andharithonu friendly ga vundadaniki try chesthaadu.
But prathi saari thanu andharithonu comedy gaane move avthaadu.
But thanu appudappudu chinna pillalaaga behave chesthuntaadu.

Good habits:-
-> Girls tho matladetappudu konchem limits loo vuntaadu.
-> Thanaku edhaina manasulo adagalanipisthe ventane adigesthaaru, endhukante thanu manasulo emi dhachukovalanukoodu.
-> Thanaku helping nature kuda ekkuve.
-> Thanu edhaina work chesthunte mathram chaala serious ga thisukuntaadu.

Bad habits:-
-> Thanaku emaina adagalanipisthe ventane adigeyakunda konchem edhutivaallu feel avuthaara lera ani konchem think chesthe better ani naa feeling.

Nick name:- "The Friendly Jester" (meaning:- his ability to stay friendly while often bringing humor to the group).
